Founder & Backend Developer — PetalCart

I am a backend-focused developer who built PetalCart to solve a practical problem — helping local shops manage and sell their products digitally. I enjoy working on the logic behind applications, structuring databases correctly, and ensuring systems behave reliably in real-world use.

In developing PetalCart, I handled the complete backend workflow. I designed the PostgreSQL database schema, implemented user authentication and authorization, developed cart and order processing logic, and integrated Razorpay for secure online payments.

The application is built using Django and deployed on Render, with Supabase managing the PostgreSQL database. Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in relational data modeling, backend request handling, role-based access control, and understanding how production deployment differs from local development.

I focus on writing clean, structured, and maintainable backend code. My goal is to build systems that are practical, scalable in design, and easy to extend as requirements evolve.
